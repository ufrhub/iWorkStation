About Us

iWorkstation is an online and offline education and training provider. It is involved in consulting for the Architecture, Engineering and Construction sector. A place where students will be trained and assisted to get placed in a company or be employed.

Vision

To provide a platform for people to learn and grow in the field of Digital Engineering.

Mission

To mature the Architecture Engineering and Construction Industry by connecting the Industry and Academics.
