PANEL MEMBERS


Chirag Dedhia

Chirag holds an architecture degree from Mumbai University & also a post graduate diploma in business management. Chirag founded Studio4 in 2007 creating a huge awareness of BIM softwares in India and abroad, and has been instrumental in developing a domestic resource pool of BIM experts. Chirag has guided and mentored leading Architectural & Engineering firms, Builder/Developer companies in their journey of using BIM. Prior to BIM consulting, Chirag has also led and successfully managed Studio4’s outsourcing business . Chirag has attended the Autodesk University and was invited as a speaker at BIM events. Chirag was invited to present at the AU 2015 India & SAARC event.


Nagaraju Joopaka

Nagaraju Joopaka holds a degree in Mechanical Engineering from a leading university in India and Diploma in Mechanical Engineering. His overall experience in the Engineering & BIM - Services Industry spans over 21 years driving growth, across major Markets both International & National. He had successfully managed many BIM projects along with Customers from Various verticals such as Commercial, Residential, Pharmaceutical, Healthcare, Government, Airports and Metro Rail. He had worked with different Companies as the Chief Technical Officer and holds P&L responsibility for company’s Global business.


Syed Mohammed Nadeem

Nadeem had been a part of many reputed organizations like Mercury Engineering (Qatar), Al-Naboodha (UAE), ETA (UAE), Telematics (Oman) and Aditya Birla Group (India). With 12+ years of experience in Revit & BIM projects. In maximum forms, He was appointed as company's first BIM Employee and my responsibility were to setup best practices for BIM Implementation for the projects and originations. He was assigned to train internal staff and managing the daily task within the BIM department / Client and Monitoring the progress of each project to ensure that the BIM team achieves the targets. He have developed, implemented, trained, maintained the BIM Protocol and BIM Collaboration Procedures in the projects.


Adnan Ghori

Adnan is a young 24 years old Digital Change agent, working towards BIM Implementation and education in India. He is the Founder & Director of iWorkstation. He has also worked for India BIM Association as an active member, Techture Structures, FSIX BIM Solution and MS Architects. He holds a Diploma and a Bachelors Degree in Civil Engineering. He is also a Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM) by PMI, an Autodesk Certified Professional and senior technician at Institution of Engineers India. He has worked on BIM projects from India, Middle East, Canada and UK. He has trained more than 1500+ students and professionals in India.


Animes Fyz Sarkar

Animes holds a degree in Civil Engineering from Biju Patnaik University, Odisha. Having about 15 years of experience of managing BIM for the AEC Industry across the globe, he specialises in starting BIM Teams from scratch. After working extensively both as a Service Provider and as a Client, he approaches BIM from a Lateral perspective and believes in Tailored Solutions for Clients. Presently he is working in the capacity of a General Manager for BIM Services with Pan Gulf Technologies. He is also an avid motorcyclist and a DIY enthusiast, Carpenter, Plumber & Electrician for his own projects.


Sohail Rizvi

Sohail Rizvi is a qualified engineer, experienced in front ranking BIM platforms like Autodesk Revit(Architecture/Structure/MEP), Tekla Structures, Civil3D, Navisworks, Primavera, Ms Project, Computation BIM Environemt -Dynamo, Game Engines like Unreal and many more. His forte lies in BIM implementation across all professions in design, engineering, procurement & construction as well as the overall life-cycle, from inception to demolition, of a built asset.
