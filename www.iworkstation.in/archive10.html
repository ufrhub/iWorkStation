POOJA YOGESHA

We at iWorkstation believe in making the design world a better place and spread knowledge as much as possible, with this perspective we generated the idea of INDIA BIM SERIES. One of the personalities who made our ideas of sharing expert knowledge with people a real time story was MS. POOJA YOGEESHA, she is currently pursuing masters in Civil Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, Italy. She has gained considerable knowledge in the field of Structural Engineering & interacting with the International forum on a day-to-day basis, she is exploring ways to contribute to the field of research in Civil Engineering through BIM. Open to possible futuristic opportunities in the BIM sector. Further, the research focuses on establishing AECO organizational BIM strategies with a key focus on maturing and aligning the professionals & their efforts towards organizational BIM vision & mission.

Topic: LCA of building.
Subtopics covered in the lecture were:

  • LCA necessity, calculation, available software.
  • Methodology and analyzing product.
  • Greenhouse gases.
  • Role of environment.
  • Embodied energy.
  • Energy conscious design of facade.

  • Building performance analysis procedure.
  • Simulation performance analysis tool.
